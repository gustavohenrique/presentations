1. Learning English

Most of the time the people start a new english course to be able to improve their knowledge. Every year a company called Linkedin makes a quiz about what the most important competency to know. Many participants say that speak in english is the most important ability to get a good job. I agree to an extent because a good experience is so important as speak in english.

2. Travel Arrangements

Incredible places to visit can be found by people using a computer. The internet is the best way to learn about your destination. Also you can book the cheapest hotels. Once a while is more cheap to make a reservation in a hotel via internet than via telephone.

3. Urbanization

Some big cities have cameras distributed along of them to provide important informations for the citizens. In Rio for example there are cameras on top of a beautiful large round building located in commercial area that provides traffic information.

4. Habits and Routines

Most of the employee's daily life starts when their alarms goes off and after getting up, they sets off drive to work or gets on the bus. In some cities is possible to see via cell phone how intense is the traffic and see the buses schedules.

5. Cooking

If you cook then probably have the benefits given by technology. The more sophisticated stoves allow you to prepare a wonderful dinner. For example, you can broil beef, roast potatoes and boil eggs. Take a peeler, a knife, a frying pan and make delicious French fries.
